We're B-Side Book Club. For over a year now we've been an A Cappella Group in the Portland, Oregon area. We are the proud winners of The Great Figgy Pudding Caroling Competition of 2016. We prefrom at weddings, work functions, and parties/events. Singing is a beautigul gift and we love sharing it! Contact us to book us!

B-Side Book Club is a small mixed-voice A Cappella group comprised of friends who love to sing together, based out of Portland, OR.

B-Side Book Club A Cappella emerged from a group of close-knit friends who all shared a love for singing. All of the group members participated in collegiate A Cappella during their undergrad, and wanted to continue singing together beyond graduation. B-Side Book Club has been a place of solace, creativity, and musical inspiration for all of its members.
